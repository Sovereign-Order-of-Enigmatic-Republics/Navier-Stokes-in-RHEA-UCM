1. Purpose

The Sovereign Order of Enigmatic Republics (SOER) operates at the intersection of computation, symbolic logic, entropy research, and recursive intelligence.
Our mission, tools, and communities must remain constructive, respectful, and intellectually rigorous.

This Code of Conduct establishes:

Expected behavior

Prohibited behavior

Enforcement structure

Reporting channels

Maintainer responsibilities

It exists to protect contributors, researchers, users, moderators, and the integrity of SOER projects.

2. Scope

This Code applies to all participants, including but not limited to:

Project maintainers

Researchers and developers

Contributors

Collaborators

Users

Sponsors

Community members

It applies in all SOER spaces, such as:

GitHub repositories

Chat systems

Issue trackers

Pull requests

Project emails

Events, livestreams, and online discussions

Private interactions directly related to SOER work

3. Standard of Behavior
3.1 Expected Behavior

Participants will:

Demonstrate respect, clarity, and professionalism

Engage in constructive dialogue

Use evidence-based reasoning

Credit the work of others

Maintain confidentiality when appropriate

Correct mistakes openly and honestly

Accept feedback without hostility

Uphold scientific rigor

Maintain the safety and dignity of all participants

3.2 Prohibited Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory remarks

Personal attacks or intimidation

Persistent disruption of conversations or work

Knowingly spreading false technical or scientific claims

Unauthorized disclosure of private or sensitive information

Doxxing, stalking, or surveillance of contributors

Unapproved redistribution of SOER intellectual property

Tampering with research artifacts, simulations, or repositories

Attempting to bypass moderation, bans, or security measures

5. Enforcement Process
5.1 Investigation

All reports will be:

Acknowledged within 7 calendar days

Reviewed impartially

Evaluated based on available evidence

Considered in context and with confidentiality

5.2 Possible Outcomes

Depending on severity and history, enforcement actions may include:

Private warning

Public warning with clarification

Temporary suspension from discussions

Temporary revocation of repository access

Permanent ban from all SOER platforms

Revocation of membership or contributor status

Invalidating or rolling back contributions that violate integrity standards

Maintainers may take any action deemed necessary to preserve community integrity and scientific rigor.

6. Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers must:

Enforce this Code consistently

Review and act on reports promptly

Protect the privacy of reporters

Avoid conflicts of interest

Model the standards described here

Foster an atmosphere of respectful, serious research

Maintainers must never:

Retaliate against reporters

Ignore patterns of misconduct

Protect violators for personal or political reasons

7. Guiding Principles

The Sovereign Order of Enigmatic Republics is founded on:

Precision

Rigor

Respect

Symbolic clarity

Recursive integrity

Truth-seeking above ego

Our conduct must reflect these principles at all times.

8. Amendments

This Code may be amended, refined, or extended at any time by:

Paul Martin Roe, Sovereign Architect of SOER

SOER Ethical Council (once officially established)

Amendments take effect immediately upon publication to the repository.

9. Acknowledgment

By participating in any SOER project, platform, repository, or discussion space, you agree to abide by the terms presented in this Code.
